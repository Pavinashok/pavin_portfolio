Automatic Car Wiper System

The Automatic Car Wiping System is an innovative solution designed to enhance vehicle automation by automating the windshield wiper function based on environmental factors like rain or dust. This system uses sensors to detect the presence of water or dirt on the windshield and activates the wiper mechanism accordingly.

Key Feature:

  • Sensor Integration: Utilizes rain sensors to detect moisture levels on the windshield and automatically triggers the wiper action when necessary.
  • Automation Logic: Developed an algorithm that adapts to different levels of moisture, ensuring optimal wiping speed and efficiency.
